Grammy's House is a non-profit organization empowering grandparents for raising children with resources, building stronger family units, and enhancing communities one family at a time.

About Me

My name is Judy Bailey. In 2009, my niece, my nephew, and a precious 3-year-old she was babysitting at the time fell victim to triple homicide. Till this day, I still don't and cannot listen to the Christmas song, "First Noel". A couple of years went on and I noticed that more mothers were losing their children to murder, to gang violence, and to drugs. I thought then that there was something I had to do to keep her memory alive. I founded Grammy's House not only to aid Grandparents with the necessary resources to effectively raise their grandchildren, but also to honor how Noelle Washington lived, and not solely by her last moments on earth.

The average cost to feed a family of four is $971.85 per month according to the USDA. Imagine trying to cover that cost on a fixed income like many grandparents in our community do every day.

What do you do when you have already downsized? Where do the grandchildren sleep? According to the CWLA, (Child Welfare of America) 103,604 local children were in the care of grandparents.
